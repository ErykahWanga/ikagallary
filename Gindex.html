IKA`S GALLARY

A DOCUMENTARY PHOTOGRAPHY

Name: Erykah Wanga

Born: Nairobi, Kenya

Profession: Documentary Photographer

Nationality: Kenyan

Erykah Wanga is a passionate documentary photographer born in Nairobi,
Kenya. Her work focuses on capturing raw, powerful stories of everyday life,
highlighting the resilience of communities and the challenges they face. Inspired by Nairobi’s vibrant street life and Kenya’s landscapes, Erykah uses her photography to tell untold stories, particularly those of marginalized people. Studying photography, she began documenting rural Kenyan communities impacted by climate change. Her emotive images have earned international recognition and her work has been featured in various global publications. Erykah’s photography not only aims to capture life as it is but also seeks to inspire change and raise awareness about critical social and environmental issues.
